Web Design

I have worked as a freelance web designer for the past several years. While balancing freelance web design with other responsibilities can be difficult and challenging, the experience on the other hand has been quite invaluable and rewarding.

I have met many interesting customers over the years who have forced me to think creatively (and work hard for every penny). Freelance web design has helped me hone my HTML programming and my graphics design skills in ways that would have been difficult to accomplish in a typical work environment, and the lessons have helped me become a better UX designer. It has also forced me to learn graphics programs such as Photoshop. While I do not consider myself an expert, I hope examples below demonstrate that I have learned enough Photoshop skills to create reasonably aesthetic visual designs. I do not create icons from scratch, but I do refer my customers to istockphotos.com where beautiful pictures, graphics, and drawings can be purchased from highly professional graphics designers at very reasonable prices.
