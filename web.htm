Web  Design

I worked as a freelance web designer for several years. While balancing freelance web design with other responsibilities was difficult and challenging at times, the experience on the other hand has been quite valuable and rewarding. Freelance web design has helped me hone my HTML, CSS, and graphics design skills in ways that would have been difficult to accomplish in a more rigid corporate design environment. Working with my own customers was also beneficial in understanding different personas and their needs.
